## Design characteristics

- 520px max width — reads like a mobile site on desktop.
- Very few blocks: hero + multilink + newsletter only above the fold.
- Typography-first, image-first minimal layout.
- Love-button and Instagram suggest a curated, social-native brand.

## Use this demo when...

- Single-product or ultra-curated catalogue.
- Artisan/designer brands where the site should feel like a magazine.
- Mobile-first brand where desktop visitors are rare.
