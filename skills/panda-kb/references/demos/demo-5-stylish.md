## Design characteristics

- Softer palette (typical shoes/leather/neutrals).
- Newsletter promoted very high — brand-building emphasis.
- Tag cloud near the footer — good for SEO-heavy niches.
- QR code in header as a mobile-to-store device.

## Use this demo when...

- Shoes, leather goods, or boutique apparel.
- Newsletter-first acquisition strategy.
- Client wants a tag/keyword cloud for SEO visibility.
