# Advanced social feed for Instagram

1. This module has permissions to get media from public content, which means you can display other users' media on your site.
2. You can get media from Ins by usernames and hashtags.
3. Media can be displayed in grid and slider layout.
4. You can create as many blocks as you want to display media from different sources.

The first thing you need to do after installation. Click on the link marked in red to get an access token from Ins, and then you can get media from Ins.

![Advanced social feed](assets/img/ins_connect.jpg)

If everything goes well, your account details will be displayed like this. A block will be automatically created to show your recent media on the top of your site.

![Advanced social feed](assets/img/ins_account.png)

###### How to get username?

Username, not name. Fill in one username, currently does not support multi-usernames. You have to click on the button right under this field after changing username to check if the username you filled is validate and get user information from Ins. See the following two screenshots.

![Advanced social feed](assets/img/ins_username_1.jpg)

![Advanced social feed](assets/img/ins_username_2.jpg)
