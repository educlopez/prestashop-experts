## Niche + target

Video-led hero. The swiper block is used as a full-width video slider instead of static imagery. Followed by featured categories grid, product-category slider, then blog and brand banners.

## Design characteristics

- Video hero sets the emotional tone — ideal for storytelling brands.
- Featured categories grid is the primary 'shop by category' entry point.
- Blog teasers mid-scroll support content marketing.
- Low use of trust-focused blocks (no newsletter by default).

## Use this demo when...

- Brand has strong video assets (lookbook, behind-the-scenes).
- Storytelling-driven site (outdoor, lifestyle, artisanal).
- Category-first navigation is more important than product-first.
