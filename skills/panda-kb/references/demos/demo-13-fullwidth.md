# Demo 13 - Fullwidth

## Niche + target

Edge-to-edge fullwidth layout — no container max-width. Hero slider flows full screen, featured categories tile immediately after, then a banner + newsletter. Designed for immersive brand sites.

## Design characteristics

- Full-bleed hero and banners — no left/right margins.
- Featured categories as a wide tile grid.
- Fewer product sliders; more lifestyle-tile blocks.
- Tag cloud near footer.

## Use this demo when...

- Brand prioritises immersive visuals (art, photography, premium retail).
- Retina/4K monitor audience.
- Client explicitly rejects the 'boxed' look.
