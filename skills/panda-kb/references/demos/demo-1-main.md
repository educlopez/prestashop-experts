## Design characteristics

- Bright multi-accent palette with coloured sale badges.
- Classic e-commerce density: many blocks on a single scroll.
- Editorial-style blog teasers mid-page (homenew + blog + featured articles).
- Heavy use of iconography in multi-links and social row.

## Use this demo when...

- Client wants a feature-rich fashion/multi-category shop out of the box.
- You need a broad canvas to remove blocks from, not add them.
- Blog-driven content marketing is part of the strategy.
