## Design characteristics

- 1440px container — suited to high-resolution monitors.
- Triple product slider in sequence: featured -> bestsellers -> special.
- Banner sits between product and blog sections as a visual break.
- Homenew block placed very high, right after the megamenu.

## Use this demo when...

- Client's audience is desktop-heavy and has large monitors.
- You want broader product shelves than the 1200 default.
- Otherwise similar brief to Demo 1.
