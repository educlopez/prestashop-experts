## Design characteristics

- Spacious, gallery-like layout with fewer blocks.
- Large hero swiper for lifestyle photography.
- Limited product-slider usage — catalogue is curated, not mass.
- Instagram in the footer to reinforce lifestyle imagery.

## Use this demo when...

- Furniture, home decor, lighting, curated lifestyle brand.
- Product range is small and image-driven.
- Client prefers 'editorial catalogue' over 'shelves of products'.
